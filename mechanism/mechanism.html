# üçç Community Network Mechanism

The community network mechanism aims to establish a robust community network through incentives and referrals, laying a solid foundation for the development of advertising businesses. The mechanism encourages active participation in the community, where efforts and contributions are rewarded reasonably. Additionally, members who have formed a certain size of community become blockchain nodes, contributing to the vitality of the decentralized advertising network and its governance.

# Explanation of Community Mechanism

# 1. New Member Enrollment

  • Each new member joins through an invitation link from a referrer, starting at level 0.

  • New members are automatically placed in the shortest and earliest registered positions in the referrer's expanded network chain.

  • Each layer of the referrer's network has a maximum of 3‚Åø positions.

# 2. Member Promotion

  • To advance to level 1, new members must transfer 100 USDT to the referrer's wallet address as a reward, and then receive their exclusive invitation link.

  • Advancing to higher levels requires:

    • a. Successfully inviting and promoting 3 level 1 members.
    • b. For each level n promotion, transfer 100 USDT to their level n+1 upline.

  • The highest level is level 9, where transactions from downline members are accepted only within 9 levels (including level 9).

# 3. Level Skipping

  • When making payments upwards, if the corresponding upper-level member does not meet the required level, the system skips to the first member who meets the level requirement upwards to maintain network continuity.

  • For example, if a member needs to upgrade to level 5 but their direct upline is only level 3, the system will skip this member and find the first level 5 member upwards.

# 4. Becoming a Blockchain Network Node

  • Members who reach level 9 become Ad-Web3 advertising blockchain nodes. These nodes play a crucial role in the Ad-Web3 token system and advertising distribution, contributing to personal earnings and the development and prosperity of the Ad-Web3 advertising business.
