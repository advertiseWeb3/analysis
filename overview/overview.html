# üçÖ Overview of Upcoming Ad-Web3 Breakout:

Ad-Web3 is a blockchain-based advertising platform that decentralizes ad space transactions using smart contracts. Advertisers can purchase ad space using platform tokens, while members can earn token rewards through participating in community games. The platform not only provides ad exposure but also incentivizes user engagement through interactions and promotional activities within the ecosystem.

# Relevant Documentation:

Token Code: ADW

Total Supply: 21 billion

Release Date: August 2024

Distribution Mechanism: Game-based community issuance rules:

  • Sending 100u, users receive 10,000 ADW
  • Receiving 100u, users receive 5,000 ADW
  • Total issuance up to 21,000,000,000

# Final Destruction:

As of UTC time November 24, 2024, 0:00, if there are any remaining tokens unreleased, all tokens will be destroyed. Ad Slot Sales Mechanism Ad slot sales are managed through smart contracts. Advertisers use ADWEB for purchases, and the contract automatically executes the following operations:

  • Destruction: 10% of ADW received by the contract will be destroyed.
  • Ad Interaction Rewards: 5% will be allocated to ad interaction rewards (media follow, retweet, interaction), attracting more merchants to list ads.
  • Staking Rewards: 40% will be distributed to users staking ADW.
  • Giver's Reward: 30% will be distributed as promotional rewards to users giving 100 USDT.
  • Recipient's Reward: 15% will be distributed as promotional rewards to users receiving 100 USDT.

Community Incentive Program Ad-Web3's unique online gaming mechanism aims to rapidly expand the community.

# Rules are as follows:

The global market is unified by a multiple of 3, forming a social network as shown in the figure. Each new member joins through an invitation link from a referrer, starting at level 0.

  • When a new member advances in level, they need to transfer 100 USDT to the referrer. They can then advance to level 1 and receive an exclusive invitation link.
  • To advance beyond level 1, they must complete corresponding referral and donation tasks, up to a maximum of level 9. Upon reaching a level, they can claim 100 USDT corresponding to their level. Members who do not reach the level will not receive gifts exceeding the corresponding level, resulting in loss of earnings.
